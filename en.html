Introduction

Join our exciting and diverse summer camp, allowing children aged 6 and above to explore the joy of multiculturalism in a comfortable environment! With a brand-new three-story school facility, professional dance studios, specialized cultural classrooms for Chinese and English enhancement courses, vibrant cultural activities, and indoor sports classes, campers will experience an unforgettable journey of growth. Children can choose dance, Chinese, or English courses based on their interests.

Summer Camp Features

  • Customized Curriculum: Designed for the Aylmer community, integrating with after-school activities and next year's summer camp for a seamless educational experience.
  • Trilingual Instruction: All courses and activities conducted in English, French, and Chinese, offering excellent opportunities for language learning.

Highlighted Features:

  • Diverse Learning: Led by experienced teachers Liya Yang, Steven Ke, and Dowin Ke. Focus on dance and Chinese learning, physical exercise, and skill enhancement.
  • Rich Curriculum: Includes dance, basic Chinese, art, science, and traditional Chinese culture activities, fostering a profound understanding and appreciation of multiculturalism.
  • Holistic Growth: Committed to comprehensive development academically, emotionally, socially, and culturally, preparing children for a globalized world.
  • Multicultural Experience: An inclusive environment for at least 15 child groups to build friendships, explore talents, and enjoy learning.
